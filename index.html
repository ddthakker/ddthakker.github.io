D. D. Thakker & Co is chartered accountants' firm registered with The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. The firm specializes on tax law. Mr. Dipak Dayalji Thakker, the proprietor, is a fellow member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India with over 30 years of experience in corporate finance, taxation, audit and regulatory matters in India.

He has held senior positions in professional firms and business, managing a range of tasks including statutory audits, internal audits, financial management, tax compliance, and regulatory compliance.

Mr. Thakker is an active member of several professional organisations.
