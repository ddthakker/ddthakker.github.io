D. D. Thakker & Co

Chartered Accountants

D. D. Thakker and company is a proprietary firm of chartered accountants registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants, India. The firm specializes in tax practice. The proprietor, Mr. Dipak Thakker, is a fellow member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India having wide experience of over 30 years in various facets of finance, taxation, audit and regulatory framework in India.

He has served in senior positions, both in professional firm and industry, responsible for various assignments covering statutory audits, internal audits, corporate financial management, tax representation and compliance, and regulatory issues of individual, firm and corporate entities in India.

Mr. Thakker is actively associated with several professional associations as a member, speaker and moderator on professional subjects.
