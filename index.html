D. D. Thakker & Co

Chartered Accountants

D. D. Thakker & Co is a chartered accountants' firm registered with The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India. The firm specializes on tax law. Mr. Dipak Dayalji Thakker, the proprietor, is a fellow member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India with over 30 years of experience in corporate finance, taxation, audit and regulatory matters in India. The firm operates from Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra.

Mr Thakker has held senior positions in professional firms and reputed companies, managing a range of tasks including strategic financial management, working capital, fund mobilisation, statutory audits, management reviews, due diligence, direct and indirect tax representation and regulatory matters.
