D. D. Thakker & Co

Chartered Accountants

D. D. Thakker & Co is a proprietary firm of chartered accountants. The firm specializes in tax law. The proprietor, Mr. Dipak Dayalji Thakker, is fellow member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India and has over of 30 years of experience in a variety of financial, taxation, audit and regulatory areas in India.

He has held senior positions in professional firms and business, managing a range of tasks including statutory audits, internal audits, financial management, tax compliance, and regulatory compliance.

Mr. Thakker is an active member of several professional organisations.
