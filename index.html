D. D. Thakker & Co

Chartered Accountants

D. D. Thakker and company is a proprietary firm of chartered accountants registered with the Institute of Chartered Accountants, India. The firm specializes in tax practice. The proprietor, Mr. Dipak Dayalji Thakker, is fellow member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India having experience of over 30 years in various facets of finance, taxation, audit and regulatory framework in India.

He has served in senior positions, both in professional firms and industry, handling various assignments covering statutory audits, internal audits, financial management, taxation and regulatory compliances.

Mr. Thakker takes keen interest in professional activities as an active member of various professional bodies.
